# eMall Project

A **multi-role e-commerce management and shopping platform** that supports **end-to-end management** of products, orders, users, and more.

---

## 1. Project Background

With the continuous growth of online shopping demand, traditional e-commerce systems can no longer meet the needs of **collaborative management among multiple roles (administrators, merchants, users)**.

This project mainly addresses the following issues:

- **Digitalization and automation** of core e-commerce processes such as product management, order processing, and user reviews  
- **Permission separation and collaborative efficiency** under multi-role operations  

**Target users:**

- Small and medium-sized e-commerce enterprises  
- Independent merchants  
- General consumers  

The project aims to comprehensively improve **management efficiency** and **shopping experience**.

---

## 2. Project Objectives

- Achieve **visualized management** of business processes including products, orders, users, and reviews  
- Support **multi-role permission separation** to ensure data security and operational convenience  

**Success criteria:**

- Stable system operation  
- Core functions covering the main e-commerce business workflow  
- Smooth user interactions  
- Accurate and reliable data
